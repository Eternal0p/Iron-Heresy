## Notes & Considerations

### Pacing
- Tutorial should be seamless, not text-heavy
- Introduce complexity gradually
- Boss should feel like a test of learned skills
- Leave player wanting more (Act 2 hook)

### Narrative Hook for Act 2
After completing Act 1, the player should:
- Understand the main conflict
- Care about the protagonist's goal
- Be curious about the antagonist
- Feel empowered by progression
- Want to continue the journey

### Replayability Considerations
- Hidden collectibles encourage exploration
- Optional challenges or alternate paths
- Unlockable lore content
- Speed-run potential
